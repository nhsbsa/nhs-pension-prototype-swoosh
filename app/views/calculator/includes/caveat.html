How we work out your estimate

This estimate is not guaranteed. It is based on current law and pay information provided by your employer.

Our website has guidance on how your estimate is calculated.

If you’re currently paying pension contributions we have assumed a pay increase of 2% each year.

We have estimated an inflation figure of 0.5% each year.

What the calculation does not include

The calculation does not include:

  • Early Retirement Reduction Buy Out (ERRBO)
  • Additional Pension
  • ‘Pensions on Divorce’ Debit or Credit that applies to you
  • Scheme Pays debits
  • benefits you’ve transferred in from a Club CARE scheme
